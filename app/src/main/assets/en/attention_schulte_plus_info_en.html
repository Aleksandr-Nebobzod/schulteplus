Improve your consciousness with Attention Schulte Plus! Our application embodies a psychodiagnostic tool created by German psychiatrist Walter Schulte, which is used in the scientific community for measuring attention, training, and developing speed reading skills. Traditionally, attention properties include stability, concentration, distribution, switching, and volume, some of which are directly recorded in each exercise result, while others are accessible through methods of indirect statistical analysis*.

The application implements the methodological foundations of Psychonetics - a technology of consciousness activation, which uses techniques for managing psychological (emotional) states, as well as cognitive processes - attention, perception, imagination, will, methods of self-regulation. The concept of careful work with discipline and minimizing competition allows practitioners to work with meta-abilities, such as second attention (in the terminology of Carlos Castaneda).

In addition to variations of Schulte tables, we are constantly developing the ecosystem, complementing it with gems from the treasury of psychotechnical and meditative materials that can be useful to everyone seeking:

  • Mindfulness
  • Self-development
  • Openness to the world

Unlike entertaining-gaming and laboratory-tests versions of "Schulte tables", our system is aimed at adaptability and content management to ensure a balance between the practitioner's pace of development and the level of workload. The system includes elements of a social network, consolidates thematic information resources, and allows interaction with like-minded people in a constructive and friendly atmosphere.
