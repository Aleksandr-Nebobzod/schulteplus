Stereo dot

The exercise uses the "Base" space, the main task here is to concentrate on the object with elements of virtuality, observing the indistinguishability of nature of things for the mind.

Instruction

In this exercise, you should split the dots: gradually obtain four dots, then three. Physically, the eyes look as if into the distance, attention gathers on the central dot and is maintained on it.
Steps from the previous exercise are applicable. Additionally, during the session, one should realize that:

  1. the middle dot is a non-existent object;
  2. other semantic contents are also non-existent objects;
  3. only the dot is perceived...

Upon completing the exercise, press the left button of the "Base" space, fill out an entry in the journal.
Realize that despite the illusory nature of objects in this world, we live in this world, interact, and can be productive and happy.

Summary

Initial encounters with illusions allow for a more mobile perception of reality, more delicately noticing the fluctuations of the mind, and with light voluntary effort directing one's energy to what is considered good for oneself and others.

[1] A variant of performing this exercise with eye convergence is possible, that is, the focus of the eyes is closer than the stimulus material (the eyes are converged inward).
