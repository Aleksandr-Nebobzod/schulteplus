Primary Flow

The exercise uses the space "Base", the main task here is to concentrate on the object with elements of virtuality, observing the indistinctness of nature of things for the mind.

Instructions

In this exercise, you need to double the dots: gradually get four dots, then three. Physically, the eyes look as if into the distance[1], attention focuses on the central dot and is maintained on it.
Steps from the previous exercise are applicable. Additionally, during the session, it is important to realize that:

  1. the middle dot is a nonexistent object;
  2. other semantic contents are also nonexistent objects;
  3. perceive only the dot...

At the end of the exercise, press the left button of the "Base" space, fill out the entry in the journal.
Realize that despite the illusionary nature of objects in this world, we live in this world, interact, and can be productive and happy.

Summary

Initial encounters with illusions allow for a more agile perception of reality, more finely noticing fluctuations of the mind, and with light voluntary effort directing one's energy towards what we consider good for ourselves and others.

[1]A variation of performing this exercise with eye convergence is possible, i.e., the place where the eyes focus is closer than the stimulus material (eyes are converged inward).
