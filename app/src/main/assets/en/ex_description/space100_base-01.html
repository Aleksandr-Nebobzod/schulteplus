Training space "Base"

The space of basic exercises offers simple, usually static visual stimulus material[1] and minimal actions.

Instruction

Controls:

  1. Center of the screen — the suggested object for training;
  2. Right button [✓] "Event";
    -- used to register various events[2] during exercise, for example:
    • progressing to the next step in the exercise structure;
    • distraction during exercise;
    • achievement during exercise;
  3. Event counter[3]
  4. Timer[3]
  5. Left button [◁] "Exit";
    -- after exercise completion, pressing this button brings up the "Results" dialog, which provides a brief summary of achievements and offers:
    • continue exercise with button [Yes]
    • end exercise without filling out the self-observation journal — button [No][4]
    • enter self-observation entry a) impressions, achievements, observations, and future questions; b) emotional state; c) energy state)
      -- button changes label to [Not][5]
Summary

Even in such a straightforward exercise space, maintaining mindfulness is not always easy. Through these simple workouts, we gain skills in mindfulness, precision, and progress.
This app element can be used with your own stimulus material (any image on Android OS can be turned into a workout with event counter, timer, and journal note by long-pressing and selecting "Send" from the menu).
Exercise results list is accessible in the "Stats" window.

[1] We have plans to expand this exercise space for audio materials and moving images.
[2] Due to the variety of registered event types, this parameter does not affect rankings.
[3] Displayed only when the "Hints" parameter is enabled.
[4] In our view, what mindfulness can there be if goals are not set and progress towards them is not tracked.
[5] Here, "No" means — Do not continue the exercise.
