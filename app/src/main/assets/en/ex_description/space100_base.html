ATTENTION SCHULTE PLUS
Methods
Let's be concise!

Space "Base"

The basic exercise space offers simple, generally static visual stimulus material[1] and minimal actions.

Instructions

Control elements:

  1. center of the screen -- the proposed object for training;
  2. right button [✓] "Event";
    --used to register certain facts[2] during the exercise, for example:
    • moving to the next step in the exercise structure;
    • distraction during the exercise;
    • achievement in the exercise;
  3. event counter[3]
  4. timer[3]
  5. left button [◁] "Exit";
    --at the end of the exercise, pressing this button will bring up a "Results" dialog box, which contains a brief summary of achievements and offers:
    • continue the exercise with the [Yes] button
    • end the exercise without filling out the self-observation journal -- button [Hell][4]
    • enter a self-observation record: a) text of impressions, achievements, remarks, and questions for the future; b) emotional state; c) energy state)
      -- the button will change its label [No][5]
Summary

Even in such a simple exercise space, it is not always easy to maintain consciousness. In these simple exercises, we acquire the skills of observation, accuracy, and movement towards self-development.
This application element can also be used with your own stimulus material (any picture in the Android OS can be turned into a training session with an event counter, timer, and journal entry by long pressing and selecting "Send to..." or "Share to..." Attention Schulte Plus).
The list of exercise results is available in the "Stats" screen.

[1] We have plans to develop this exercise space for audio materials and moving images.

[3] Displayed only when the "Status" parameter is enabled.
