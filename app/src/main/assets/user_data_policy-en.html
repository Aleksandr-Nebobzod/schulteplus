User Data and Privacy Policy

The developer of "Smart Rovers", registered on the Google Play platform, hereby notifies the user that the "Attention Schulte Plus" application (hereinafter referred to as We) does not collect your personal information: name, age, address, phone number and passwords.
We do not share your email address. We use Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication so your email address may be used subject to the conditions defined in the document at the following link https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
We recommend your to make username abstract.

The Firebase Crashlytics service may collect the following non-personal information to ensure application reliability:

  • Application instance ID
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address de-identification (approximate location data)
  • In-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Application life cycle events.
If you have any questions regarding the Privacy Policy, please contact Smart Rovers support: nebobzod@gmail.com

We do not ask for your financial information:

  • Credit card information
  • Information about the form of payment
  • Amount of any transaction
  • Total number of transactions
  • Banking information

We do not collect your device identifiers, for example:

  • Applications installed on the device.
  • Device location data.
  • Contacts
  • SMS and call log data
  • Using a microphone and camera.

We do collect from your device:

  • The size and orientation of your screen.
  • Is this device real or simulated?
The purpose of collecting this data is to provide meaningful filtering of exercise results.

We collect data about users' exercise performance and store it for the whole system lifecycle. Active users will be notified of system lifecycle events. If the user deletes his account, this data is converted into depersonalized archived state, beyond retrieve.
